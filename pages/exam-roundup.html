Exam Roundup

How mastery works

What is mastery?
Your mastery score combines practice work, in-class activity, and quiz results to measure how well you understand each topic.
This is not a prediction of your exam score
It measures how prepared you are based on what you've done so far — not what score you'll get on the exam.
Reach your target
Once you hit ★ 80+ on a Quantitative topic or ★ 70+ on a Verbal topic, you've mastered it.
How to improve
Focus on topics you haven't yet mastered. Practice sessions are the fastest way to move your score.
